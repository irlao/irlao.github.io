Introduction

The Homie

The Homie is a home goods store that will hook you up with fresh home decor that follows the latest furniture trends. We sell our own in-house brand as well as a curated selection of second-hand pieces. Whether you're looking for the perfect chaise for your vintage-inspired living room, or a sleek mirror for your modern bedroom, The Homie has your back!

Target audience

The target audeience is trendy people looking to decorate their living space.

Their primary task is to look through the furniture catalogs and purchase a piece. The goal is to easily filter through the different kinds of furniture styles and leave with something they are happy with.
